Standard Grey Resin | $26

This standard grey resin is great for beginners. It isn't to expensive and it will last you a long time. You will get high quality prints out of it as well. One thing to note is that your prints will need to be washed in isopropyl alcohol because it is not water washable. There are many different colors avaliable to purchase. Link to Standard Grey Resin

Water Washable Resin | $50

Water washable resin is a little more expensive, however it is safer for humans to be around. Since it is water washable, you do not need isopropyl alcohol. Simply wash your prints with water. Although it is safer, it is harder to achieve consistent good prints with this resin. I wouldn't recommend this to beginners. Link to Water Washable Resin

High Quality Fast Curing Resin | $75

Siraya Tech resin is some of the best resin out there. However it does come with a steep price point. This resin will give you super durable and high quality prints. This resin is commonnly used for mechicancal parts and extremely high quality mini figures. Link to High Quality Fast Curing Resin
